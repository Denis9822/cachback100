If you have not received a cashback within one hour of paying for the product, please cancel the order and place it again, carefully following the service tips.
1
Make sure you enter your country correctly
Cashback programs only work if you choose the proper country to shop from. You can change the country using the menu at the top.
2
Turn off browser extensions
Some of the extensions can interfere with the correct cashback crediting. These include Adblock, friGate CDN, Aliexpress Seller Check, extensions from other cashback services, as well as VPN/Proxy/Socks. Use a separate browser without any extensions to make cashback purchases.
3
Add items to your cart only AFTER switching from cashback100.
For the purchase of goods added to the cart BEFORE switching from cacheback100, no cashback is credited.
4
Make a purchase in a single browser session.
After closing the browser, the information about switching through the cacheback100 is deleted.
5
Close all additional tabs and browser windows before making a purchase.
It is worth clearing your browser cache.
6
We don't recommend using Opera browser for shopping with cashback.
Quick links in the express bar contain a referral code, which can interfere with the correct cashback crediting. When you click on this link, the purchase is not assigned to a cashback service, but to the Opera browser.
7
Do not use Incognito mode.
Incognito mode makes it impossible for the store to determine from which source the customer switched, so the store won't pay out any commissions.
8
Place and pay for your order from a single device.
Pay for your order right after the order is placed. Before each purchase, switch from the cashback service.
